Grub boot Tiny Core on Virtual Workstation


Yes, I am aware that I am running this in an older version of Virtual Workstation. But trust me when I say it's the same instructions. But I will try to update this as soon as possible.

If there are no instructions/image for a specific step, then take the default.

First, we'll create a new virtual machine.
Make sure to go with Custom.
Pick the disk image file. I have already downloaded Tiny Core Plus onto my Downloads folder.
You can change the name of your Virtual Machine.
I picked 1 GB since my professor recommended it. Yoy may pick your preferred amount of memory.
Select IDE.
The next screen will prompt you to select your disk. Click on: Create a new virtual disk.
This is what the last step should look like.
Right click on the Tiny Core tab. The newer version of VM has the selection of: Power On to Firmware. Click on that. This will boot up Tiny Core.
This is what the bootup should look like. Press F10 to save and continue. This won't exit/shutdown the process.
This is what the next step should look like. Press enter to select the very first option.
Yay! We're almost done!
Click on: Apps. It's at the bottom of the screen.
Click the following from the Apps program on the top left side: Apps -> Cloud (Remote) -> Browse
Type: cfdisk
into the textbox and press Enter.
Click on: cfdisk.tcz
on the left side of the program. On the bottom, change OnBoot to Download + Load
Press on the button: Go
Click on the Terminal at the bottom of the screen now.
Become root by typing: sudo su
You will not be prompted for a password. Type: cfdisk
This is what the next step should look like. Select: New
with the Tab and Enter buttons.
Select: Primary
Select: Bootable
Select: Write
Type: yes
Select: Quit
Type: fdisk -l (<- Lowercase L)
You should have something similar.
You can exit out of the programs now. Click on: TC_Install
at the bottom of the screen.
Make sure: Frugal and Whole Disk and sda
are selected. Select on the next arrow key.
Select: Proceed
Once the installation is completed, you may exit out of TC_Install.
Press: Ctrl + Alt
to use your default OS's mouse. You won't be able to do anything in TC OS until you select on its screen. Right click on Tiny Core's tab and select: Settings...
Select: CD/DVD (IDE)
Uncheck: Connect at power on
Select: Use physical drive Click: OK
Select: No
Finally, reboot Tiny Core. This can be done by selecting: Exit
at the bottom of the screen. You can also right click on TC's tab and select: Power -> Shutdown
Once TC boots up, it will now run on the Virtual Machine.
